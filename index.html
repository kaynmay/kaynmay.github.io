Rain Data

Python automation script that turned a daily 30 minute process into being fully automated. A coworker at my company had a daily task of going into DAT files and calculating the total rainfall from the last 24 hours to enter into our Asset Management system. I was tasked with finding a way to automate this process. I created a script that copied over the DAT files, calculated the total rainfall at each site, and wrote the totals to a CSV file.

Four Seasons

Processing animation project that displays the four seasons. This was the final project for my Graphics and Visualization course that was developed by two other students and myself. Each season is displayed with a pixel art representation as the background, an animation that runs on top of the background, and the appropriate section of Vivaldi's Four Seasons as audio.
